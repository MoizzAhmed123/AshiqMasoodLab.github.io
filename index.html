Welcome to Masood Lab

Indiana Univeristy School of Medicine

Mission of our lab is to elucidate the evolution of Cancer with real time data. We approach our success by developing various computational methods to analyze the data obtained by working in concert with Clinicians and pathologists. Using cutting-edge bioinformatics technologies we characterize various complex molecular subtypes of intratumoral heterogeneity.
